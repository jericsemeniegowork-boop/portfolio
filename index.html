Representative QA workflow

This reflects how I approach validation work in enterprise-style support and testing environments.

01

Acknowledge and review

Take ownership of the ticket or request, review the details, confirm the ask, and capture the initial notes.

02

Verify and replicate

Investigate the issue, reproduce it in a controlled environment, and document what conditions trigger the behavior.

03

Document and align

Record steps, results, screenshots, and observations, then confirm with the requestor that the issue understanding is correct.

04

Support approval and release

Prepare validation evidence, help move the change through approval, and recheck the behavior in production after deployment.

05

Confirm and close

Send the release confirmation, upload supporting files, complete the documentation, and confirm whether any update is still needed.
